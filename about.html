About Ocean City Heating and Cooling

We are an independent HVAC Refrigeration company that services Vancouver and the Fraser Valley. Our number one goal is quality, and creating a financially efficient experience for your home or building including retro fit of aging equipment or design solutions to fit your needs.

Our team of mechanical/electrical engineers and certified technicians are proficient at installing, repairing and maintaining advanced hvac and refrigeration equipment. You can rely on us to deliver superior service and solutions. We specialize in offering tailored installation services such as ductless split ac, central air conditioning, heat pumps and boilers, roof top ac, HRV ventilation, make up air and exhaust. As well as commercial refrigeration such as walk in coolers freezers and ice machines.
